Lucky Egg Sorry What

Lucky Egg Sorry What is a hilarious and fun lip reading game that's perfect for family gatherings or group game nights. Designed for social interaction, it encourages laughter, teamwork, and entertainment in every round.

The game includes custom-made headphones that play distracting music, making it harder for players to focus on the spoken words. With a built-in timer, you'll need to guess the phrases quickly, creating a lively and amusing atmosphere. It's ideal for all ages, from kids to adults, and makes for a great icebreaker at parties or team-building events.

Each game comes with 100 random word cards, ensuring endless rounds of unpredictable and funny challenges. The headphones are safe and comfortable, with a maximum volume of 85dB, making it suitable for children and adults alike. Everything you need is included: headphones, cards, a charging cable, and easy-to-follow instructions.

Whether you're hosting a family dinner, a birthday party, or simply looking for a fun way to engage friends during game night, Lucky Egg Sorry What is the perfect choice. It turns conversations into comedy and keeps everyone entertained, making it a must-have for any social event.
